GartenPlaner is an offline-first Android app for year-based garden planning.

Plan your garden year in a clear calendar view: which plant is sown, transplanted, harvested, or cared for in which month? The monthly overview shows all activities at a glance — color-coded by activity type.

Features:
- Multiple garden plans (one plan per year)
- Plant library with 37+ entries in 6 categories (fruiting vegetables, root vegetables, leafy greens, herbs, legumes, onions & leeks)
- Add and edit custom plants manually
- Sections for structuring your plan (e.g. "Vegetables", "Herbs")
- Climate zone and frost data per plan
- PDF export via Android print dialog (A4 landscape)
- JSON backup and restore via file storage
- Copy a plan to the next year

Privacy:
- No internet connection required
- No ads, no tracking
- No Google services
- All data stays on your device
